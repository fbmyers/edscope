Welcome to CellScope!

Though not visible with the naked eye, a vibrant microscopic world of life sustains us, makes us sick, and balances our ecosystem. Optical microscopes provide access to that world, but high quality microscopes are typically limited to well-equipped laboratories. To address that limitation, the Fletcher Lab at the University of California, Berkeley has developed a simple attachment that turns a cellphone or tablet computer into a fully-functional microscope that we call CellScope. We apply CellScopes for a range of applications in clinical diagnostics, global health, environmental monitoring, and education. The CellScope Explorer app has been specifically designed for CellScope, enabling users to capture microscopic photos and videos and share these with the world.
